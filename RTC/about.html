TESDA History

TESDA Regional Training Center - CALABARZONwas established to implement Technical-Vocational Education and Training (TVET) programs and projects needed by the people and communities and be a productive citizen of the nation.

The TESDA RTC - CALABARZON offers a wide variety of programs, including higher level training programs (NC III and IV ) and Diploma Programs, particularly in the sectors of Automotive, Electrical & Electronics, Metals and Engineering, Heating-Ventilating, Airconditioning and Refrigeration Technology. Construction, Agriculture and Fishery, etc. In the years 2014, 2015, & 2016, TESDA RTC - CALABARZON was constituently recognized as the Best TVET Training Institution in CALABARZON and in the year 2022 became a finalist for the National Best TVET Training Institution.

Under the System for TVET Accreditation and Recognition (STAR), Automotive Servicing NC II, Pipefitting NC II, Electrical Installation and Maintenance (EIM) NC II, Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Servicing (DomRAC) NC II, Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW) NC II, Mechatronics Servicing NC II, Organic Agriculture Production (OAP) NC II and Gas Tungsten Arc Welding (GTAW) NC II have been recognized because of excellence in curriculum.
